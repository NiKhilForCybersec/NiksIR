Management Reports

What Management Wants to Know

  • Are we protected? (threat landscape)
  • Are we efficient? (SLA, response times)
  • What's improving? (trends over time)
  • What do we need? (resource gaps)
  • Any significant incidents? (risk awareness)

Weekly Summary Structure

  1. Executive Summary: 2-3 key points
  2. Metrics Dashboard: Visual trends
  3. Significant Incidents: Brief summaries
  4. Improvements: What's gotten better
  5. Concerns/Needs: Resource or coverage gaps
Keep It Visual

Use charts and graphs for trends. Executives prefer visuals over walls of text. Show week-over-week or month-over-month comparisons.

Key Takeaways
  • Different audiences need different report formats
  • Daily reports maintain operational continuity
  • Track key metrics consistently over time
  • Management wants business impact, not technical details
  • Use visuals to communicate trends effectively
